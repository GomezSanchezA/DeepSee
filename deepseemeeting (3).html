DeepSee Meeting 2025

Advanced Fluorescence Microscopy: From Single-Molecule Sensitivity to High-Speed 3D Tissue Imaging

About DeepSee

The DeepSee project is developing a very high-performance fluorescence microscope that can deliver single-molecule sensitive and extremely fast 3D imaging of tissues and tissue mimics, located within the facilities of the host research group.

The instrument will enable transformative investigations such as in-tissue superresolution imaging and fast 3D studies of dynamic processes. The resulting capability can be directly exploited in a broad variety of well-established and emerging research fields, including:

  • Life and material sciences
  • Spatially-resolved 'omics' (transcriptomics, proteomics, etc.)
  • New approaches for emitter multiplexing
  • 'Smart' imaging
  • Nanoparticle monitoring

This meeting brings together leaders in the field to discuss advancements, applications, and future directions of this groundbreaking technology.
