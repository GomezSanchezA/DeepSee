From Single-Molecule Sensitivity to High-Speed 3D Tissue Imaging

About the DEEPSEE Scientific Meeting

The DEEPSEE Scientific Meeting is a conference for exploring high-resolution imaging, advanced spectroscopy, and chemometric techniques. From single-molecule insights to tissue-scale imaging, we invite experts to share novel approaches, breakthroughs, and collaborations.


Through invited talks, and in-depth discussions, this meeting will spark innovative research directions in areas such as fluorescence microscopy, superresolution imaging, and computational analysis. Join us to discover how cutting-edge microscopy and chemometrics can revolutionize molecular imaging.

Venue Information

Polytech Lille Amphitheater

Avenue Paul Langevin
Cité Scientifique
59655 Villeneuve d'Ascq
France

The meeting will be held in the amphitheater at Polytech Lille, offering modern facilities and audiovisual equipment to support our scientific discussions.

The venue is easily accessible by public transportation from Lille city center. Detailed directions will be provided to registered participants.

Expected Attendance:

The meeting is aimed at researchers in advanced microscopy, spectroscopy, and related fields. We anticipate approximately 50-70 attendees, including invited speakers, researchers from the collaborating laboratories (LASIRE and Leuven), and other interested scientists.
